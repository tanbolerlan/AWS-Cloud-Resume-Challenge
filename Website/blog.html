From Biology to Bytes: My Journey into the Cloud

Embark on a digital odyssey with Tanbol – a former biology aficionado turned cloud engineer! From decoding bladder cancer mysteries to mastering the language of code, join me in a captivating journey where biology meets bytes, and every line of code tells a tale of transformation.

Greetings,

As I usher in the new year, I'm excited to share a bit more about myself and the unexpected twists of my professional journey. I'm Tanbol, and if you're wondering how a biology enthusiast ended up navigating the vast landscapes of technology, buckle up for a quick ride into the clouds.

My academic journey took me from being a biology geek to delving deep into the realms of Pharmacology and Toxicology for my master’s. As I penned a thesis on bladder cancer, the limitations of traditional wet lab work in biomedical research became apparent. That's when I decided to hack the system.

Venturing into programming and bioinformatics, I discovered the transformative power of coding – an unsung hero in our modern world. Post-master’s, I immersed myself in learning Python, algorithms, databases, and Genomic data science. With no prior Computer Science background and minimal stats experience, the learning curve felt like mastering a new language. But challenges only fueled my determination.

After a stint as a Teaching Assistant and a detour into medical device sales for a Chinese company in Kazakhstan, I found myself at a crossroads. The constant travel, bureaucratic hurdles, and a saturated market led me to seek a new horizon.

Fast forward to November 2023 – I summoned the courage to embark on a new chapter as a cloud engineer. And this is where my personal website comes into play. It's more than just a static resume; it's a digital diary chronicling my highs, lows, and discoveries as I navigate the clouds.

So, welcome to my digital space, where biology meets bytes, and I document the chapters of my cloud journey. Join me on this adventure filled with serverless wonders, code snippets, and the thrill of mastering a new set of skills.

Cheers to the journey ahead!

Tanbol

Navigating the Cloud: My AWS Certification Journey

Embark on a riveting adventure as I unveil my journey to conquer AWS certifications—where excitement meets challenges in the cloud's intricate labyrinth. From decoding serverless wonders to embracing the AWS Solutions Architect Associate realm, this blog post is a thrilling chronicle of navigating the digital skies, acknowledging that certificates are but a compass in the boundless landscape of cloud expertise.

Hey Cloud Enthusiasts,

Buckle up as I unravel the exhilarating adventure of studying for AWS certifications—a journey marked by excitement, challenges, and a profound commitment to mastering the cloud.

Having recently earned my AWS Cloud Practitioner and Solutions Architect Associate certifications, the thrill of delving into the intricacies of cloud computing is still palpable. From the foundational AWS Cloud Practitioner exam to the more complex Solutions Architect Associate certification, each step was a leap into a realm where serverless wonders and scalable architectures awaited.

The excitement was infectious, but it came hand-in-hand with challenges. AWS exams are no walk in the park. With each certification, I faced the daunting task of mastering an extensive array of services, understanding architectural best practices, and applying my knowledge to real-world scenarios. The complexity blew my mind, much like the intricate paths of a labyrinth in the digital realm.

However, every challenge was met with an equal measure of determination. Late nights deciphering AWS documentation, hands-on labs to solidify theoretical concepts, and countless practice exams became the norm. The process wasn't just about passing exams; it was a journey of self-discovery, pushing boundaries, and honing a skill set that's shaping the future of technology.

Yet, it's crucial to acknowledge that certifications alone don't define expertise. They're a stepping stone, a compass guiding us through the vast AWS landscape. What truly matters is the hands-on experience, the ability to architect robust solutions, and the agility to adapt to the ever-evolving cloud ecosystem.

In essence, certifications are a valuable tool for learning and practicing the knowledge gained. They provide structure to our cloud education, allowing us to systematically explore the vast AWS services. It's not just about obtaining a badge; it's about embracing the mindset of a cloud architect, understanding the nuances of cloud design, and gaining confidence in navigating the AWS console.

So, if you're contemplating the AWS certification journey, remember that the value lies not just in the certification itself, but in the learning process, the practical application of knowledge, and the continuous pursuit of excellence in cloud architecture.

Here's to the cloud journey—a path of challenges, triumphs, and the relentless pursuit of knowledge!

Cheers,

Tanbol

From Cloud Certifications to Code: My Odyssey with the Cloud Resume Challenge

Dive into my latest blog post where I spill the beans on my transition from AWS certifications to coding wizardry in the Cloud Resume Challenge. Join the exhilarating journey of decoding HTML, CSS, and JS, embracing the trials, victories, and the sweet satisfaction of conquering the coding cosmos.

Hey Cloud Enthusiasts,

I recently embarked on the Cloud Resume Challenge, a thrilling odyssey on AWS that took me from certified cloud explorer to novice programmer and designer. Buckle up as I share the highs, lows, and the exciting journey of delving into the world of HTML, CSS, and JS without a prior clue.

The challenge kicked off with a seemingly insurmountable task for an inexperienced coder like me—designing a functional website. As someone who didn't even know what HTML, CSS, or JS stood for, the journey began with deciphering the alphabet soup of web development. The struggle was real, but so was the excitement of unraveling the mysteries behind the front-end wizardry that powers websites and web applications.

The coding journey was a rollercoaster of trial and error, late-night Google searches, and the occasional frustration of a misplaced semicolon. From the initial confusion of divs and spans to the moment when I realized the power of JavaScript in making pages come alive, every line of code became a victory.

Surprisingly, the other aspects of the challenge felt like a walk in the cloud, thanks to my experience during the study for the Solutions Architect certification. Navigating AWS services, understanding architectural principles, and optimizing for scalability were realms I had recently conquered, making these components of the challenge a breeze.

This blog post is a candid account of my transformation from a certified cloud professional to a fledgling web developer. It's a testament to the resilience required to learn a new language—the language of code—and the satisfaction that comes with seeing your ideas take shape on a webpage.

So, if you're contemplating the Cloud Resume Challenge or diving into the coding universe, know that the journey is as valuable as the destination. Embrace the challenges, relish the victories, and savor the sweet taste of success when that first line of code brings your website to life.

Cheers to the cloud, the code, and the unwavering pursuit of new horizons!

Happy coding,

Tanbol
